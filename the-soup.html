The Soup

Description

The soup is a tortellini, spinach, creamy tomato and chicken soup popularised by Reddit.

The soup requires a slow cooker, and the original recipe contains meat, however this version has options for vegetarians.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium white onion, diced
  • 40g cup plain flour
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 litre vegetable broth (note: orignal uses chicken broth)
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 500g vegan chicken substitute (e.g. Quorn Chicken Pieces), jackfruit or tofu
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1-2 packs cheese tortellini (frozen or fresh)
  • 1 medium pack raw spinach, washed
  • 200g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 240ml double cream

Method

  1. Mix onion, flour, basil, garlic, tomato paste and a drizle of olive oil in a microwavable bowl.
  2. Microwave the mixture for 5 minutes, stirring every 90 seconds.
  3. Optional: dry fry chicken substitute to remove moisture, especially if frozen.
  4. Add the mixture, as well as the broth, tomatoes, chicken substitute, salt and pepper to the slow cooker and stir together.
  5. Cover and cook on Low for 4-6 hours, or High for 3-4 hours.
  6. Add tortelli, spinach, Parmesan cheese and pre-warmed cream (this is to avoid curdling)
  7. Cook on High for about ten minutes.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
