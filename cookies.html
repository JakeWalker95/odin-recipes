Chocolate Chip Cookies

Crunchy yet soft chocolate chip cookies, made with demerara sugar to add a delicious brown sugar taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 125g butter, melted then cooled (still liquid)
  • 125g demerara sugar
  • 125g granulated sugar
  • 250g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped into small pieces

method

  1. Sift the flour and baking powder into a mixing bowl and mix together.
  2. In a separate mixing bowl, beat the egg, then add the vanilla esence and butter and mix together.
  3. Add the demerara sugar to the egg mixture and mix well, then do the same with the granulated sugar.
  4. Alternately add small amounts of the flour and the chocolate chunks to the mixture, mixing well each time.
  5. Use a dessert spoon to take enough of the mixture to make small balls of cookie dough. Pace on a greaseproof paper-lined baking tray.
  6. Bake the dough balls in an oven preheated to 190C/fan 170C/gas mark 5 for 7-10 minutes. The cookies should look slightly underdone with only a hint of the golden brown crust when you remove them from the oven.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking tray for 10 minutes before moving them onto a wire rack to finish cooling
